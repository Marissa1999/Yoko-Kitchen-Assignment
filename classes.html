Samurai Sushi

12-hour Advanced Class in Tokyo

This intensive cooking class will allow students to train and learn the tips and necessities to master various recipes with various tastes such as delicious types of sushi: maki, temaki, uramaki, sashimi and nigiri. Rika Hairi will show the right procedure to do so.

Top Japanese Cook-Off

One Week Class in New York

Do you think you are the master chef and want to improve on your skills? Well, this class has the ability to teach anyone how to master traditionally ancient dishes with the famous Yoko Ono, herself!

Succulent Side Salads

One Day Regular Class in San Francisco

A simple cooking class, where fresh soups, salads and cool beverages are being concocted and chopped at ease. With a wide range of delicious vegetables and calming spices, students will be able to cook with Yoshito Shin, the wise and quiet one.

Decadent Desserts

Two-Week Training in Osaka

After a delicious main course meal, many people are looking forwards towards the sweeter side of food: dessert. This class teaches the main basics of creating light after-tastes such as chichi dango. With the secret ingredients including rice and cane sugar, these confectioneries would sure satisfies one's cravings.

Bold & Spicy

One Day Intensive Course in San Antonio

This is an intensive one-day course that allows tough and advanced students to learn the specific techniques about creating the best spicy dishes, that can satisfy anyone's explosive taste buds from the mighty Takeo.

Deep & Juicy

One Week Workshop in Ottawa

Dive your taste buds into temptation, by learning the basics of quick, yet delicious tempura dishes. Yoko Ono will lead her students the way, by showing the main steps of concocting special soy oils and smooth seafood mixtures.

Ravishing Noodles

One Month Program in Chicago

This long program will allow students to master the main basics of cooking rice and bean noodles, as well as savory sauces that can please anyone's taste. Takeo will gladly demonstrate how to make the perfect slurping dishes.

Meet the Masters

Yoko Ono

Known for teaching various competitions across the globe, a peace activist and as the founder of this website, Yoko is extremely well-known and proud to teach anyone who wants to cook authentic Japanese cuisine dishes, that date back during the Asian Dynasty era, hundreds of years ago.

Yoshito Shin

Known for creating delicious and nutritious soups and salads, Shin envisions that healthy cooking is beneficial for the body and soul. Take his Succulent Side Salads class, because your body and mind will thank you for his meditative and efficient techniques while cooking in a simple manner.

Rika Hairi

Known for being attentive and bright, Hairi is most famous for creating a variety of decadent sushi, right before your very eyes. Taking her appetizer class will allow the students to learn about various safe and quick chopping techniques, to speed the cooking process.

Nori Toyo

Known for appreciating the hot tastes of various spices, Toyo is a strict, but excellent professor for students who love to dedicate their time towards intensive and focused cooking. He will teach anyone how to combine various flavours and spices, to create mind-blown spicy creations for many dishes.

Shima Takeo

Known for taking pleasure for one's sweet tooth, Takeo has the capability to have absolute patience for students who really enjoy various addictive desserts. Take a walk on the sweet side, by paying constant attention during his Decadent Desserts class. Your taste buds will unravel with absolute delight.
